JustNature is a company specializing in plant-based sanitizers and disinfectants. During the pandemic, JustNature wanted to bring out two different types of disinfectants that could be carried out at all times. The design focused on clearly communicating the benefit of the plant-based disinfectant, which does not damage the skin like conventional alcohol-based disinfectants. The design was executed using typography and was designed as a dispenser, when upright, that could be used at the home or the office. The two different disinfectant packages retained the consistent design for the Just Nature brand.
